How AI Sees Through Synthetic Faces

Modern deepfake detectors analyze micro-inconsistencies invisible to the human eye — blinking artifacts, unnatural skin textures, and lighting gradient mismatches that GAN-generated faces consistently produce. Our model processes every frame independently, building a statistical fingerprint of authenticity.

Blockchain Hashing: The Future of Media Provenance

SHA-256 creates an unbreakable link between a file and its origin. Once a video is scanned and its hash registered, any modification — even a single bit flip — produces a completely different hash, making tampering immediately detectable without needing the original file.
